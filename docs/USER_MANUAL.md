# CCMS Staff User Manual

This manual is written for staff using CCMS during daily operations.

## 1. Login

Open the frontend, enter your username and password, and sign in. For local development only, the default account is `admin` with password `Admin123`.

## 2. Change Password

Use the user menu in the top bar and choose Change Password. Use a strong password and keep it private.

## 3. Dashboard

The dashboard shows high-level totals, operational status, and alerts. Use it as the starting point for daily review.

## 4. Child Records

Use Children to search, add, edit, and review child profiles. Use “Not recorded” fields as a cue to complete missing information.

## 5. Admission Documents

Upload admission-related documents from the child profile. Verify documents when the original record has been checked.

## 6. Accommodation

Use accommodation screens to manage buildings, rooms, beds, and bed allocations. Transfer or vacate beds only when the operational change is confirmed.

## 7. Attendance

Daily attendance records the child’s organization presence. Education attendance records school attendance separately.

## 8. Medical Records

Record medical profiles, visits, medications, vaccinations, and related documents according to staff access level. Viewer users may see limited information.

## 9. Education Records

Maintain school enrollment, academic year, class level, results, and education attendance.

## 10. Case Management

Use case profiles, notes, reviews, incidents, counseling, and care plans to coordinate welfare follow-up. Confidential or restricted notes are protected by role permissions.

## 11. Sponsorship

Create sponsor profiles and child sponsorship records. Sponsorship ownership is preserved for historical accuracy.

## 12. Visitors

Register and verify visitors before visits. Block visitors when required by policy.

## 13. Child Visits

Schedule, approve, check in, check out, complete, or cancel visits. Record safety notes where appropriate.

## 14. Child Development

Record observable development information, strengths, interests, support needs, and staff follow-up actions.

## 15. AI-Assisted Development Summary

Generate summaries from recorded observations for human review. Staff should review the summary before relying on it for planning.

## 16. Behavior Support Plans

Create or generate support plans using positive, observation-based language. Review plans regularly and record progress notes.

## 17. Reports and PDF Exports

Use report and export buttons to download approved PDF or Excel files. Viewer exports are masked where required.

## 18. Logout

Use Logout from the user menu when leaving the workstation.

## 19. Safe data handling notes

- Do not share passwords.
- Do not export sensitive information unless authorized.
- Do not upload personal test files into demo databases.
- Keep printed reports secure.
- Report incorrect or missing records to an authorized manager.
